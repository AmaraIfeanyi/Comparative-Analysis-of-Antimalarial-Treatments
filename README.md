# Comparative-Analysis-of-Antimalarial-Treatments
This analyzes and compares effectiveness of different Antimalarial Treatments 
📊 Comparative Analysis of Antimalarial Treatments

##Overview

This project presents an interactive dashboard developed to analyze and compare the effectiveness of different antimalarial treatments using clinical study data. The dashboard provides insights into mortality outcomes, recovery indicators, treatment performance across demographic groups, and regional variations.

The goal of the project is to transform complex healthcare data into meaningful visual insights that can support evidence-based decision-making and facilitate a deeper understanding of antimalarial treatment outcomes.

---

##Problem Statement

Malaria remains one of the leading causes of morbidity and mortality in many parts of the world. Healthcare professionals and researchers require reliable analytical tools to evaluate the effectiveness of available treatments and identify patterns that can improve patient outcomes.

This project addresses that need by providing a comparative analysis of antimalarial treatments through interactive visualizations and performance metrics.

---

##Objectives

- Compare mortality rates among different antimalarial treatments.
- Evaluate treatment effectiveness using recovery-related indicators.
- Analyze treatment outcomes across age groups.
- Examine geographical differences in treatment performance.
- Identify trends and patterns in mortality over time.
- Provide an interactive platform for exploring treatment data.

---
##Dataset Overview 

The dataset contains information collected from multiple antimalarial treatment studies and includes variables such as:

- Treatment/Drug Type
- Mortality Rate
- Patient Age Group
- Geographic Region (Continent)
- Route of Administration
- Parasite Clearance Time (PCT)
- Fever Clearance Time (FCT)
- Coma Recovery Time (CRT)
- Study Year

##Key Findings

The analysis reveals notable differences in treatment outcomes across antimalarial drugs. Mortality rates vary between treatments, suggesting differences in effectiveness under specific conditions. Recovery indicators such as parasite clearance time and fever clearance time provide additional insights into treatment efficiency.

Age-group analysis highlights variations in treatment performance among adults and children, while regional comparisons demonstrate how treatment outcomes can differ across continents.

These findings can support healthcare researchers, policymakers, and practitioners in evaluating treatment effectiveness and identifying areas for further investigation.
